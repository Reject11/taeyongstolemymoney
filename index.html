Shipping & prices

Shipping Info
If I send your letter and have a proof that I did hand it to the delivery service I'm not responsible if it gets lost.
If the post office loses it then they are responsible.
I will try all I can to get in contact with them and get a refund (if it was insured) but if I don't get money from them I will not refund you from my money.

Shipping in Germany starts at 1€ for PCs and 1,60€ for bigger items.

Shipping International starts at 1,70€ for PCs and 3,70€ for bigger items.

If the shipping goes outside of the European Union everything that is thicker then a postcard or PC has to be shipped as a package because of customs!
If you are not sure if that's the case for an item you're interested in don't hesitate to DM me.

If you buy something from me I will let you know the shipping options and prices you can choose from.
If you want your items insured please let me know so that I can check for that.

I don't have fixed shipping days!
Mostly I send on the weekend.
I try to send everything as soon as possible but send at least after around 2 weeks.
Standard, tracked letters and packages can be send on different days since I have to different options to send them.

GO Information

General information:

For every GO I will create a Instagram group chat.
I will share all updates I get in the group chat and you can always check for the current staus at my notion list/masterlist.
If you want updates in between feel free to always ask me.
If the post don't say anything else I will do member sorting for random PCs.
No Backout!
If you don't pay or fill out forms until the deadlines I will resell "your" item after a reminder by DM!
I can't guarantee that the GO package from my supplier to me has the full value on it.
So if it gets lost (never happend before) I will most likely not be able to fully refund you.

Member Claims
If I do member claims it's for shops that say they will send out full sets when you order the amount of albums you need for a Set.
(Example: NCT Dream - 7 Albums, Ateez - 8 Albums, etc.)
It can still happen that I don't receive a full set. If that's the case I will include the POBs in the Sorting!

Packaging Fees:
For all GOs I take a small packaging Fee.
  • Small letters: 0,60€
  • Medium letters: 0,80€
  • Lager letters: 0,90€
  • Package: depending on size and packaging
  • Each fee includes one toploader, every additional one: 0,15€
Depending on the GO they can be higher.
If you have any questions about it don't hesitate to DM me.

Shipping the GO:


Around the time that the GO items are send I will send you an address form so that I can finish the packaging fast.

Shipping without tracking:

Member sorting:

!Changed System for all new GOs starting from 31.08.2023!
All GOs with random PCs will have member sorting unless it's stated otherwise!
After the GO is orders I will send different options in the group chat where you can vote for a date and time when I will drop the sorting form. The option with the most votes will win.
On the chosen time I will drop the sorting form so that all of you have the same chance to get their member wishes.

For the sorting I will use the system fcfs (first come first served).
That means that I check for your wishes in the order you filled out the form.
So I will start with the first person based on timestamps and give you the first wish that I have members available from.
Then the second person, etc.

So as faster you are with filling out the form as higher is your chance to get the member you hope for.
